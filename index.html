Smart Choice

Agent & Customer Workflows

1

Obtain New Lead

Receive lead via email and check if the agent requests that you contact the customer directly. All Smart Choice leads should be sent to mailto:CLSmart@nationwide.comCLSmart@nationwide.com.

2

Review Eligibility

Examine the submission to determine if the risk is eligible. When in doubt, lean towards gathering more information or contacting the agent rather than declining outright.

3

Gather Information

Make outbound calls to agents to get basic information needed for quoting. If calls go unanswered, follow up with emails. Full underwriting questions can be sent via email along with an indication.

4

Quote the Risk

Prepare a quote using the correct codes (NB 61650, Producer Ashley Wingate) and verify owner inclusion/exclusion rules for Workers‚Äô Compensation. Gather any required forms.

5

Present Quote

Send the approved quote email with the proposal attached. Note any pre‚Äëbind or trailing documents and read the conditional quote statement before presenting the rate.

6

Bind Request

Agents should reply to the approved quote email with answers to bind questions. They may also call to provide this information. Ensure they have reviewed payment instructions if they haven‚Äôt sent a bind email.

7

Bind Coverage

Update effective and expiration dates, mailing and billing addresses, and issue the policy based on the agent‚Äôs selected payment option. Enter payment details directly into the system, send the REFT form via DocuSign, and email the bind confirmation to the agent (and customer if requested).

8

Pre‚ÄëRenewal Direction

From the Account Summary screen, set a pre‚Äërenewal direction to refer the account to a customer service representative. Instruct the CSR to update the agency code to 57850 at renewal while keeping the producer the same and to contact mailto:CLInside@nationwide.comCLInside@nationwide.com for post‚Äëbind first‚Äëterm questions.
